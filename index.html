What is a vaccify certificate?

Vaccify certificate is a SARS-CoV-2 vaccination credential used to prove your status of vaccination.

How does vaccify work?

First, you download the Vaccify wallet and create a DID. Second, a certificate is issued by an accredited issuer, and you will receive the certificate in your wallet. The last step is the sharing of the certificate with a verifier.

Can it help post-COVID society?

As we are already experiencing, post-COVID world is radically different from the times before the pandemic. The people who have been vaccinated would need to prove their immunity so they can continue participating in the world affairs. We are anticipating a dire need in the future for COVID immunity credentials.
